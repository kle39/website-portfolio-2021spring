User-Centered Design

Midterm Project

In this course, the first half of the semester was dedicated to understanding the design process. The midterm group project was to create a mobile site for a fake business called Yappy Hour - a bar and pet adoption. Each step in the design process is incorporated amongst each component listed, starting with personas and ending with the final prototype.

Personas

Personas are created, potential users. Each group member chose a persona to create. For this project, I created the persona named Lindsay, a friendly, retired cat lover.

Scenarios

The scenarios corespond to each persona and identify the purpose of the user visting the site around and the possible steps taken to accomplish their purpose.
